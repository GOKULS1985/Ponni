Who We Are

Named for a river that
never stops giving.

The Ponni is the Tamil name for the Kaveri — a river that has fed fields, founded towns, and connected communities for centuries. We took the name as a working principle, not just a logo: build land that keeps giving back to the people who live on it.

Every Ponni development is DTCP-approved, clear-titled, and planned around how a neighbourhood actually grows — wide roads first, shade trees before show, drainage before driveways. We are land developers, but we measure ourselves by the families who eventually call a Ponni plot home.
